Instructions:

  1. At the start of the game, you'll see a random number in the Crystals to Collect section.
  2. You'll collect crystals by clicking on the crystal kaleidoscopes below. The value of each crystal is hidden from you until you click on it.
  3. By clicking on a crystal you will add a specific amount of points to your Number Collected score.
  4. To win the game, match your Number Collected to the random Crystals to Collect number. But be careful! You lose the game if your total number of crystals collected is higher than the random number.
  5. Each time a new game starts, a new value will be assigned to each crystal.
